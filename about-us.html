Our Story
Hi!

INDALIO was founded with one purpose — to improve the way India works. We design and manufacture ergonomic monitor arms, cable organizers, and workspace accessories that are durable, smooth in movement, and built to enhance productivity at every desk. From our manufacturing unit in Ludhiana, Punjab, we bring together engineering precision, modern design, and long-lasting performance — proudly Made in India.
